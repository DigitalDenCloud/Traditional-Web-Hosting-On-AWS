About

I am a Cloud DevOps Engineer from London and currently work at Digital Futures. I love working on new projects in my spare time and also experimenting with new techniques and tools. On this website you will find a few practical blogs/labs I have made for a range of topics including DevOps, Serverless technologies, Cloud Security and more.

Prior to to the cloud in my previous life, I have worked as an Immigration caseworker where I assisted clients' with Human Right Claims. I have also worked as an Account Manager where I cultivated and nurtured enduring relationships with diverse clientele.

Outside of work I have a love for sports, fitness, and thrilling experiences. I am a dedicated football player in a Sunday league team, actively maintaining fitness levels and exploring adrenaline-filled track days on my motorcycle during the summer months.
