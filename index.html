About

I am a Cloud DevOps Engineer from London. I love working on new projects in my spare time and also experimenting with new techniques and tools. On this website you will find a few practical blogs/labs I have made for a range of topics including DevOps, Serverless and Security.

Prior to the cloud, I worked as an Immigration Lawyer where I many assisted clients with their Human Right Claims. I have also worked as an Account Manager where I cultivated and nurtured enduring relationships with diverse clientele.

Outside of work I have a love for sports, fitness, and thrilling experiences. I am a dedicated football player in a Sunday league team playing CM, and enjoy exploring adrenaline-filled track days on my Daytona 675r (see above).
