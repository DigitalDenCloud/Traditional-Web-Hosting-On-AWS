About

I'm Deniz, a Cloud Engineer based in London.

In my free time I build on AWS, experimenting with various cloud services, integrating them to produce practical solutions. I manage a YouTube channel, 'Hands-On with Digital Den', where I create video demonstrations showcasing real projects built on AWS. Moreover, I contribute as a technical writer on Medium, where I publish articles under 'AWS in Plain English'. My aim is to simplify intricate AWS concepts, making them accessible to a broader audience.

Prior to the cloud, I worked as an Immigration Lawyer where I assisted many clients with their Human Right Claims. I have also worked as an Account Manager at Cool Chile Co, a company that specialises in Mexican food products, particularly tortillas, chillies, and spices.
